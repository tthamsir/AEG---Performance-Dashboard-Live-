Methodology. Figures aggregate daily transaction data from the per-deal ALE tabs (“ALEs ▸” onward); the Combined Portfolio rollup is excluded. Calendar basis groups by actual transaction date and ties out to the Database MTD / YTD reference tabs; Fiscal basis follows the 4-4-5 retail calendar from the source. The weekly view shows calendar week-ending dates on Calendar basis and fiscal week numbers (Week 1, 2, 3…) on Fiscal basis, each compared to the matching prior-year week. Revenue is net adjusted collections; avg ticket = revenue ÷ encounters. Months below 75% of the peak reporting panel are flagged partial, since later periods are incomplete as locations report on a rolling basis. Variance = current − prior. The dashboard runs on an embedded snapshot by default; when a live endpoint is configured it reads the Google Sheet directly and automatically reflects new deals, locations, and ALE tabs.
